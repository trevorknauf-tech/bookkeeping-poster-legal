# Privacy Policy — bookkeeping-poster

_Last updated: 2026-05-15_

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how bookkeeping-poster ("the Application") handles data. The Application is owned and operated by Knauf Family Office for internal use only.

## 2. Data Accessed from QuickBooks Online

With the user's explicit OAuth consent, the Application accesses:

- Chart of Accounts (account numbers, names, IDs)
- Vendor and Customer records (names and IDs)
- Department / Location records (names and IDs)
- The user's QuickBooks Online company ID (realm ID)

The Application creates, reads, and verifies financial transactions (Purchase, Deposit, Transfer entries) on the user's behalf in QuickBooks Online.

## 3. Data Storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the user's own computer in a file (`tokens.json`) with restricted file-system permissions. Tokens are not transmitted to any server other than Intuit's own authentication end
